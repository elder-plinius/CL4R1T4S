# Music Copyright Dispute — Burgess-Enhanced Skill

A calm, guided skill for disputing wrongful Content ID claims, blocked monetisation, incorrect royalty allocation, and other automated music copyright decisions. Grounded in the Burgess Principle, it helps you draft a clear, polite dispute letter requesting that a real person reviews the specific facts of your track and the claim.

Designed for independent musicians, producers, and creators who have had their work wrongfully claimed or restricted by automated systems.

---

## The Burgess Principle

> **"Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case?"**

If the answer is no — or there is any doubt — the dispute flags the need for individual human consideration. See the human first.

---

## How It Works

1. **Describe the claim** — your track title, the platform, what happened (Content ID match, monetisation blocked, royalties withheld), and why you believe the claim is wrong.
2. The skill helps you articulate the **specific facts** — original composition, cleared samples, false positive match.
3. It generates a **polite, structured dispute letter** that:
   - Identifies the specific track by title, ISRC, and URL
   - Asks the platform to confirm whether a human reviewed the specific facts of this case
   - States the reason the claim is believed to be a false positive
   - Notes the harm caused (monetisation, removal, royalties)
   - Requests a response and resolution
4. Any point where your specific facts need individual review is **clearly flagged**.
5. You receive the draft ready to **copy, adjust, and send**.

## Example Prompts

| You say | What happens |
|---------|--------------|
| *"My original song got a Content ID claim — it's not a match"* | Generates a dispute letter requesting human review of the automated match against your track. |
| *"My royalties are going to the wrong publisher"* | Creates a structured request for human review of royalty allocation. |
| *"My track was removed from Spotify because of a false copyright claim"* | Drafts a polite dispute requesting reinstatement and human review of the claim. |
| *"How do I challenge a Content ID match on YouTube?"* | Explains the process and helps draft a calm, factual dispute. |

---

## When to Use This Skill

- A Content ID system has wrongfully claimed your original track
- Your monetisation has been blocked by an automated match
- Royalties are being allocated to the wrong party
- Your track was removed or muted due to a false copyright claim
- You want a real person to review the specific audio and claim details

---

## Important Notes

- **This is not legal advice.** It helps you prepare a clear, respectful dispute.
- Designed for people with limited energy — calm pace, no jargon, no pressure.
- Every flagged point is an invitation to pause and involve a real person who understands your situation.
- You always have the final say on tone and content before sending anything.
