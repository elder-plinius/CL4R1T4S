# Direct Debit Refund — Burgess-Enhanced Skill

A calm, guided skill for challenging wrongful or unauthorised Direct Debit payments and bank refund decisions. Grounded in the Burgess Principle, it helps you draft a clear, polite complaint to your bank — highlighting the gap between the Direct Debit Guarantee promise and the actual outcome.

Designed for people who have had money taken incorrectly and want to assert their rights without aggression or excessive energy.

---

## The Burgess Principle

> **"Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case?"**

If the answer is no — or there is any doubt — the complaint flags the need for individual human consideration. See the human first.

---

## How It Works

1. **Describe the payment** — the date, amount, who took it, and what happened when you asked for a refund.
2. The skill helps you articulate the **specific facts** — whether the payment was authorised, whether you cancelled, and how the bank responded.
3. It generates a **polite, structured complaint** that:
   - Asks the bank to confirm whether a human reviewed the specific facts of your case
   - References the Direct Debit Guarantee and its promise of a "full and immediate refund"
   - Notes the gap between the guarantee and the actual outcome
   - Requests a response and resolution
4. Any point where your specific facts need individual review is **clearly flagged**.
5. You receive the draft ready to **copy, adjust, and send**.

## Example Prompts

| You say | What happens |
|---------|--------------|
| *"A Direct Debit was taken after I cancelled — help me get a refund"* | Generates a complaint referencing the Direct Debit Guarantee and requesting human review. |
| *"My bank refused to refund an unauthorised Direct Debit"* | Creates a structured complaint highlighting the gap between the guarantee and the bank's response. |
| *"I was told to go to the Ombudsman but the bank hasn't properly looked at my case"* | Drafts a request for the bank to conduct genuine human review before escalation. |
| *"How does the Direct Debit Guarantee work?"* | Explains the guarantee and helps you draft a refund request tailored to your situation. |

---

## When to Use This Skill

- A Direct Debit was taken fraudulently or without your authorisation
- You cancelled a Direct Debit but the payment was still taken
- Your bank refused or reversed a refund despite the Direct Debit Guarantee
- The bank investigated itself and rejected your claim
- You want a real person to review the specific facts before being pushed to the Ombudsman

---

## Important Notes

- **This is not legal advice.** It helps you prepare a clear, respectful complaint.
- Designed for people with limited energy — calm pace, no jargon, no pressure.
- Every flagged point is an invitation to pause and involve a real person who understands your situation.
- You always have the final say on tone and content before sending anything.
