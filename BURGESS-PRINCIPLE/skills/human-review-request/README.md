# Human Review Request — Burgess-Enhanced Skill

A general-purpose skill for requesting that a decision — automated or otherwise — be reconsidered by a real person who has reviewed the specific facts of your specific case. Grounded in the Burgess Principle.

Designed for any situation where you suspect a decision was made without individual human consideration: insurance claims, benefits decisions, automated rejections, customer service escalations, or any process where your personal circumstances may not have been seen.

---

## The Burgess Principle

> **"Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case?"**

If the answer is no — or there is any doubt — you have the right to ask. That is the whole point: see the human first.

---

## How It Works

1. **Describe the decision you want reviewed** — what happened, who made the decision, and why you believe it needs a second look.
2. The skill generates a **polite, structured request** that:
   - States clearly what decision you are asking to be reviewed
   - Asks the recipient to confirm whether a human personally reviewed the specific facts of your case
   - References any applicable rights (e.g. right to human review under GDPR Article 22 for automated decisions)
   - Suggests a reasonable next step
3. Any point where your specific facts need individual attention is **clearly flagged**.
4. You receive the draft ready to **copy, adjust in tone, and send**.

## Example Prompts

| You say | What happens |
|---------|--------------|
| *"My insurance claim was rejected — I want a human to review it"* | Generates a polite letter requesting human reconsideration of the claim decision. |
| *"An automated system denied my application — can I ask for a person to look at it?"* | Produces a request citing the right to human review of automated decisions. |
| *"I was rejected for a service and I don't think anyone looked at my case properly"* | Drafts a calm, respectful request for individual reconsideration. |
| *"How do I ask for a human review of a benefits decision?"* | Explains your options and drafts a request tailored to your situation. |

---

## When to Use This Skill

- An automated system made a decision about you
- You received a generic rejection that doesn't reference your specific circumstances
- You suspect a decision was made by algorithm or template without individual review
- You want to escalate politely and ask for a real person to look at your case
- You need to invoke your right to human review under GDPR Article 22 or similar legislation

---

## Important Notes

- **This is not legal advice.** It helps you prepare for meaningful human review.
- Designed for people with limited energy — calm pace, no jargon, no pressure.
- Every flagged point is an invitation to pause and involve a real person who understands your situation.
- You always have the final say on tone and content before sending anything.
