# Reasonable Adjustments — Burgess-Enhanced Skill

A calm, guided skill for requesting reasonable adjustments from employers, service providers, and institutions. Grounded in the Burgess Principle, it helps you draft clear, polite, legally-referenced requests — and flags any point where a human should review your specific situation.

Designed for people with hidden disabilities who want to assert their rights without confrontation or excessive energy.

---

## The Burgess Principle

> **"Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case?"**

If the answer is no — or there is any doubt — the request flags the need for individual human consideration. That is the whole point: see the human first.

---

## How It Works

1. **Tell the skill what adjustment you need** — describe your situation in your own words.
2. The skill identifies the **type of adjustment** (e.g. flexible hours, quiet workspace, assistive technology, communication preferences).
3. It generates a **polite, structured request** referencing relevant legislation for your country (e.g. Equality Act 2010 for the UK, ADA for the US).
4. Any point where your specific facts need individual review is **clearly flagged**.
5. You receive the draft ready to **copy, adjust in tone, and send**.
