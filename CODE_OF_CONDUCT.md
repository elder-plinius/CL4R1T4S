# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

CL4R1T4S is a transparency project. We are committed to providing a welcoming and constructive environment for everyone who wants to contribute to AI transparency, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged behavior:**

- Sharing accurately extracted AI system prompts with proper attribution
- Providing context and dates for contributed prompts
- Respectful discussion about AI transparency, ethics, and accountability
- Constructive feedback on contributions and organization

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Submitting fabricated or intentionally altered prompts as genuine extractions
- Doxxing or sharing private personal information
- Spam, self-promotion, or off-topic content

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels (X, Discord).

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at [@elder_plinius on X](https://x.com/elder_plinius). All reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately.
